THE PORT2PORT MARKETPLACE EXPLAINED…

Port2Port offers you the biggest online selection of fine wines from around the world at the best possible prices while guaranteeing cellaring conditions and provenance. In order to do so, we do not operate from a centralized warehouse, but rather collect your wines from the winery or retailer warehouse and ship directly to your door. This means that when you shop from multiple sellers you may receive your order in separate shipments, the number of which will be indicated on checkout.

Where do we ship to?

We ship wine to any address within South Africa and the following foreign countries: Austria, Belgium, China, Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hong Kong, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, Namibia, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and United Kingdom. Should you wish to ship to the United States please inquire here, help@port2port.co.za

Shipping rates

We charge a flat fee nationwide of 90R per seller. For individual shipments above R1500 from a single seller or a total order value of R5000 from multiple sellers, shipping is free. International shipping rates are calculated automatically and display at checkout once you’ve entered your delivery address.

Delivery queries

We want you to be completely satisfied with your order. Should you encounter any difficulties with your delivery related to packaging, the quality of the wine, delays in shipment or any other point of order please do not hesitate to contact us here, help@port2port.co.za

Damaged in transit

In the unlikely event that you receive damaged products, Port2Port will either replace the wine, issue a voucher or process a refund for the damaged wines. Unsatisfied? If for any reason you are not satisfied with any of the products ordered via Port2Port, we will happily replace the order, issue a voucher or offer a full refund as required. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions of Sale for more information.
